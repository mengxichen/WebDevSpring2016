Search Engine 

            · 	Rank the vendors/ Service provider based on its ranking, if there is no ranking history, rank by name alphabetically

        Administrator
        · 	Register as Administrator
        · 	Approve vendor or service provider based on their company credit and background check


        Vendor/Service provider
        · 	Register as vendor or service provider
        · 	Create different types of appointments
        · 	Confirm appointment and set up the price
        · 	Provide discount for loyal customers
        · 	Send text message to clients for reminder


        Customer
        · 	Search for nearby services based on zip code
        · 	Request a new appointments and choose payment method
        · 	Make payment through credit card or bank account
        · 	Make reviews(fill up the form) and rate after appointment
        · 	Refer to friends
        · 	Repeat appointment automatically
